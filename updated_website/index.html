About

Legend has it that a peasant farmer named Gordias fulfilled a prophesy by driving into the city of the Phyrgians on an ox-cart and so became their king. To commemorate the event, the ox-cart was tied to a post with an intricate, tightly entangled knot that could not be untied for several centuries. The man to unravel the knot was prophesized to become the ruler of all of Asia. Upon his arrival, Alexander the Great, after some unsuccessful struggle with the knot, drew his sword and sliced the knot in half with a single stroke and later went on to conquer Asia.


The name Gordian is based on the the Gordian Knot metaphor. In particular, movement traces have a similar entangled characteristic as the Gordian knot. The problems that Gordian solves are also (seemingly) intractable and Gordian provides effective and elegant solutions to such problems. By untangling knots of movement traces, Gordian unleashes sustainable transport solutions.

Charging infrastructure planning

Charging infrastructure planning is extremely complex! There are many components and stakeholders that have to work together while focusing on their own and societal objectives. Moreover, the optimality of any charging infrastructure plan heavily depends on the transport energy demand that the charging infrastructure components serve. Movement traces are paramount in evaluating the benefits of a placement and dimensioning of components in a complex system, which then need to be weighed against the costs.

We at Gordian provide planning tools that are are powered by lightning-fast analytics and artificial intelligence and allow to interactively evaluate or optimize a plan to optimally serve / cover the transport demand (movement traces) with a set of infrastructure components. A non-optimal plan and a optimal plan is illustrated in the grey and orange images for a single movement trace; now imagine millions of traces. There are also an astronomical number of possible placements and dimensioning of components. The speed and information in our analytics allow unprecedented optimizations: we accurately evaluate the effects of millions of the top plans against millions of movement traces under a few minutes. Thereby, we provide evidence-based decision support for single or multiple stakeholders and objectives.

Transport hub planning

Transport and transfer hubs both for people and goods are most effective if they are located at places that are along many movement traces and are either close to where the traces start, end or merge. We at Gordian believe that the keyword is along because using a hub that is off a given movement trace ultimately represents a detour which one logically wants to minimize.

We at Gordian provide planning tools that are are powered by lightning-fast analytics and artificial intelligence that allow to interactively evaluate or optimize a plan to optimally serve / cover the transport demand (movement traces) with a set transport hubs. A non-optimal plan and a optimal plan for a hub (shown as a circle) is illustrated in the grey and orange images for four movement trace; now imagine millions of traces. There are are also an astronomical number of possible placements and dimensioning of hubs. The speed and information in our analytics allow unprecedented optimizations: we accurately evaluate the effects of millions of the top plans against millions of movement traces in the order of seconds / minutes. Thereby, we provide evidence-based decision support that increases the utility of the planned hubs.

Data processing

Scalable and accurate map matching of movement traces.

Movement trace data is big, complex, and messy. We at Gordian know movement traces and provide tools and services so that you do not have to deal with this and can simply utilize your movement data assets in your core business. For example, we provide consultancy, APIs, and private-cloud based solutions to map match movement traces of millions of objects in a scalable and steaming fashion.


Knowledge discovery and management

Movement pattern discovery and management, visual analytics, data compression and anonymization.

Movement trace data contains regularities and patterns, e.g., frequent routes / route parts and dominant regional movements. We at Gordian provide tools and services that help you extract these patterns and discover new explicit knowledge about the movements. We provide visualization tools to help you gain a deep understanding of the patterns and their evolution. We also provide tools that use these patterns to compress and anonymize movement data with minimal information loss.

Transport planning and optimization

Easy, interactive, and evidence-based planning. Up to 5X more cost-effective plans than competitors.

Movement traces contain up to 10X more information than simple movement statistics. The additional information is essential to better plan and optimize resource allocations when making important decisions about transport infrastructure and operations. We at Gordian provide tools that are powered by lightning-fast analytics and artificial intelligence, and consider all parts of the movement traces. The speed and information of the analytics allow us to optimize a wide range of combinatorial transport planning problems and find plans that are up to 5X more cost-effective than those that can be found by the tools of our competitors.
