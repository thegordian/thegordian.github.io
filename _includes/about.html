About

The name “GORDIAN” is based on the metaphor that is associated with the Gordian Knot. In particular, movement traces have a similar entangled characteristic as the Gordian knot. The problems that GORDIAN solves are also (seemingly) intractable and GORDIAN provides effective and elegant solutions to such problems. By untangling knots, GORDIAN unleashes sustainable transport solutions. GORDIAN aims to fulfill a prophesy similar to Alexander the Great.
